Frequently Asked Questions

What is AreYouAlive?

AreYouAlive is a wellness check-in app that helps you stay connected with loved ones through regular status updates and automated check-ins.

How do check-ins work?

You can set up automated reminders to check in at your preferred intervals. If you miss a check-in, your designated contacts will be notified.

How do I add emergency contacts?

Go to Settings > Emergency Contacts in the app. You can add multiple contacts who will be notified if you miss your scheduled check-ins.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the app settings under Account > Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously and use industry-standard encryption to protect your information. Your check-in data and contact information are stored securely.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. AreYouAlive collects only the information necessary to provide check-in services and emergency contact notifications. We never sell your personal information to third parties. Your check-in history and contact details are stored securely and are only used for the app's core functionality.
