About AreYouAlive

AreYouAlive is a personal safety application that automatically alerts your emergency contacts if you fail to check in within a specified time. It's a digital "dead man's switch" designed for peace of mind.

How It Works

Simply set a countdown timer. If you don't check in before it expires, the app automatically sends an alert to your chosen emergency contacts via SMS. The app will also send you reminders before the timer runs out.

Key Features

  • Countdown Timer: You decide the check-in interval.
  • Emergency Contacts: Add trusted contacts to be notified.
  • Automated Alerts: If you miss a check-in, we alert your contacts.

Privacy and Security

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We only collect data that is essential for the app to function, such as your name and emergency contact details. This information is used solely to provide the check-in and alert services. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required to deliver the service (e.g., sending SMS alerts).
